SOEHARTO

A man with thousands good and thousands bad

  • President of Indonesia for straight 32 years (1966-1998)
  • Initiated a mediation between Vietnam and Cambodia by declaring Jakarta Informal Meeting (JIM)
  • Highly respected in ASEAN
  • The Father of Development with his legendary Five Years Development Plan named REPELITA
  • Revived Indonesia from economic slump
  • Achieved food self-sufficiency for Indonesia and provided tons of rice for Africa
  • Successfully suppressed the worth of US Dollar for only Rp378
  • Made a rapid economic growth in Indonesia
  • Minimized the number of crime in Indonesia with his mysterious marksmen called "Petrus"
  • Provided relatively cheap necessities
  • Decreased Rupiah's inflation rate
  • Settled Indonesia's foreign debts
  • Declared the first ASEAN Summit in Bali
  • Launched Palapa A1 Satellite as the very first satellite launched in Southeast Asia
  • Committed the ratification of Indonesia's territory through the Exclusive Economic Zone
  • Launched the Independent Family Planning to control population growth rate in Indonesia
